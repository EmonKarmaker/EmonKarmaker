## 📝 Research & Publications

### 🏥 **Deep Learning-Enhanced OCT Image Analysis Pipeline**
*Integrating Denoising, Super-Resolution, and Fuzzy Logic for Improved Clinical Diagnostics*<br>
**Accepted at ICDSAIA 2025** &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Tech**: Python, OpenCV, CNN, ESRGAN, Fuzzy Logic<br>
Built an OCT pipeline with noise reduction, LAB-based fuzzy contrast enhancement, and ESRGAN super-resolution. Trained a CNN in TensorFlow/Keras achieving **99% B-scan** and **92% volume accuracy**, running **32% faster** than baselines.<br>
🔗 [SpringerLink – ICDSAIA 2025 Proceedings](https://link.springer.com/10.1007/978-3-032-11335-1_19)

<br>

### 🏥 **A Deep Learning Based Approach to Identify Colon Cancer from Endoscopic Images**
**Published at 4th IEEE BECITHCON 2025** &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Tech**: GoogLeNet, ResNet, YOLOv8<br>
Benchmarked GoogLeNet, ResNet, and YOLOv8 on the Kvasir-SEG dataset using uniform preprocessing. Evaluated accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 — finding **YOLOv8 highest at 97.5%**, outperforming ResNet (93.7%) and GoogLeNet (90.6%) for colorectal polyp detection.<br>
🔗 [IEEE Xplore – Paper](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/11504178)
